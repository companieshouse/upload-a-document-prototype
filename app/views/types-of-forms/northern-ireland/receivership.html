How to upload 3.04, 3.05 and 3.12 documents

To upload form 3.05 with form 3.04, you must:

  • select form '3.05' from the list
  • merge the documents so that you can upload them as a single PDF
  • place form 3.05 at the front when you merge the documents, followed by form 3.04

To upload form 3.12 with an administrative receiver's report, you must:

  • select form '3.12' from the list
  • merge the documents so that you can upload them as a single PDF
  • place form 3.12 at the front when you merge the documents, followed by the report

To upload form 3.12 with form 3.04 and an administrative receiver's report, you must:

  • select form '3.12' from the list
  • merge the documents so that you can upload them as a single PDF
  • place form 3.12 at the front when you merge the documents, followed by form 3.04, then the report
Help with merging documents

To merge these documents, you could:

  • use an online tool or app that merges PDF documents
  • create a new document and combine saved files into a single PDF
  • scan documents together if you have access to a printer or photocopier, and save the scan as a single PDF
